What it is: A manuscript diagnostic that reveals what a story is already doing well, what may be holding it back, and what opportunities are waiting beneath the surface.
What it is not: Editing, automated scoring, AI-generated feedback, traditional critique.
Positioning distinction: This is diagnosis before editing. The method evaluates manuscripts against declared form, not generic standards. A lyric novel is not penalized for slow escalation. A thriller is not praised for restraint.
